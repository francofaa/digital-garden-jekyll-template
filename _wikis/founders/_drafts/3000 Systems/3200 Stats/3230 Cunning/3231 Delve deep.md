---
title: 3231 Delve deep
---
[[3230 Cunning]]

When you delve deep into strange happenings, roll +cunning. 10+, hold 2; 7-9, hold 1. Spend a hold to ask one of the following: 
- What happened here?
- What did this?
- What can it do?
- What can hurt it?
- Where did it go?
- What was it going to do?
- What is being hidden here?
